Accomodations

Our resort offers many great rooms, which you can bundle with a spa treatment at a discount! Please call us if you would like to book connecting rooms, as they do fill up quickly.

Types of Rooms

  • Single
    • Our single bed rooms can accomodate one to two people and come with a queen or king bed.
    • Amenities include a mini fridge, free cable tv, free wifi, an alarm clock, a coffee maker, and continental breakfast.
  • Double
    • Double bed rooms can accomodate two or three people. They come standard with two queen beds.
    • Amenities include a mini fridge, free cable tv, free wifi, an alarm clock, a coffee maker, and continental breakfast.
  • Master Suite
    • Master suites are our pride and joy. They have 2 rooms both equipped with king beds and can accomodate 4 people. The rooms are connected by a living room with a kitchenette.
    • Amenities include full size fridge, free wifi, free cable tv, kitchenette stocked with cooking implements, two loveseat couches, surround sound 4k tv, coffee maker, and a butler, if you so desire.
